CredCo: MisinfosecWG

CredCo creates credibility standards to help with fighting misinformation; the misinfosec working group is building TTPs so we have working infosec-style standards for describing large-scale misinformation attacks. Here’s what we’re doing in the misinfosecWG: https://github.com/credcoalition/community-site/wiki/MisinfoSec-(The-Intersection-of-Misinformation-and-InfoSec (TL;DR: 6 months of analysing attacks, pulling out TTPs, putting them in a framework and writing that up). Here’s how to join - https://github.com/credcoalition/community-site/wiki/Getting-Started (DM Sara in Slack so she can look out for you and bring you into the misinfosecwg).
