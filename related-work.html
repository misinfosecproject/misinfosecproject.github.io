CredCo MisinfosecWG

The Credibility Coalition's Misinfosec Working Group (MisinfosecWG) is our sister group, working on the standards needed to share information about and counter misinformation incidents. MisinfosecWG's first output is the AMITT TTP-based framework, as a first step towards working infosec-style standards for describing large-scale misinformation attacks.

Sofwerx: Countering Weaponized Misinformation

Sofwerx is a platform established under a Partnership Intermediary Agreement between USSOCOM and DEFENSEWERX to help USSOCOM solve challenging Warfighter problems. They have... an interest... in countering misinformation incidents. Sofwerx misinfosec activities included running the Countering Weaponised Information Summit, and working on misinfosec, AMITT etc.
